Arms

Overview

The arms consist of multiple muscle groups that enable a wide range of movements. Strong arms are essential for daily activities—from lifting objects to performing intricate tasks. Maintaining arm strength contributes to overall upper body fitness.

Nutrition Tips

Consume lean proteins and vitamin-rich foods to help build and repair muscle tissue. Eggs, lean meats, and legumes are excellent choices. Adequate protein supports muscle growth and recovery.

Exercises

Bicep Curls

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart holding dumbbells. Curl the weights towards your shoulders while keeping your elbows close. Lower slowly and repeat for 10–12 reps.

Tricep Dips

Using a stable chair or bench, place your hands behind you. Lower your body by bending your elbows until you feel a stretch in your triceps. Push back up to complete one repetition.

Arm Circles

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and extend your arms to the sides. Rotate your arms in small circles to warm up and increase blood flow. Perform for 30 seconds in each direction.
