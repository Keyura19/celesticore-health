Back

Overview

The back is a complex structure made up of muscles, bones, and nerves that support nearly every movement. A healthy back is crucial for posture, balance, and overall strength.

Nutrition Tips

Include foods rich in calcium, vitamin D, and protein to strengthen your bones and muscles. Dairy products, leafy greens, and lean meats are beneficial. Adequate hydration and a balanced diet play important roles in back health.

Exercises

Supermans

Lie face down on a mat with arms extended. Lift your arms, chest, and legs off the ground simultaneously, engaging your back muscles. Hold briefly before lowering back down.

Rows

Using a resistance band or dumbbells, bend forward slightly and pull the weights towards your torso. Squeeze your shoulder blades together as you row, then slowly return to start.

Reverse Flyes

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, holding dumbbells. Bend at the hips with a flat back, then lift the weights out to the side. Focus on contracting your back muscles before lowering.
