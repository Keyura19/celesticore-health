Head & Neck

Overview

The head and neck are critical for protecting the brain and supporting essential functions like vision, hearing, and speech. They house sensory organs and play a vital role in overall coordination.

Nutrition Tips

Focus on foods rich in antioxidants, omega-3 fatty acids, and vitamins to support brain function and reduce inflammation. Blueberries, walnuts, and fatty fish are excellent choices. Staying hydrated and balanced with whole grains can also promote optimal performance.

Exercises

Neck Tilts

Begin by sitting or standing with a straight back. Slowly tilt your head to the side until you feel a gentle stretch along your neck muscles. Hold for 15â€“20 seconds and repeat on the other side.

Chin Tucks

Sit upright and gently draw your chin toward your neck. You should feel a stretch along the back of your neck. Hold this position for a few seconds before releasing.

Shoulder Shrugs

Lift your shoulders towards your ears slowly and hold for a few seconds. Release them back down smoothly, feeling the tension release. Repeat 10 times for optimal relief.
