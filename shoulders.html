Shoulders

Overview

Shoulder muscles enable a full range of arm motion and contribute significantly to upper body strength. They support nearly every movement you perform with your arms and help maintain proper posture.

Nutrition Tips

Include a balanced diet with lean proteins, healthy fats, and carbohydrates to support muscle repair. Foods such as chicken, quinoa, and green vegetables are ideal. Hydration and vitamins are also crucial for muscle recovery.

Exercises

Overhead Press

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart while holding dumbbells at shoulder level. Press the weights upward until your arms are fully extended, then lower them slowly. Focus on engaging your shoulder muscles with each rep.

Lateral Raises

Hold a dumbbell in each hand at your sides. Slowly lift the weights outwards to shoulder height and then lower them with control. Repeat for several repetitions to build strength.

Front Raises

With dumbbells in hand, lift your arms in front of you until they reach shoulder height. Keep the motion controlled and avoid swinging the weights. Lower them back down and repeat for a full shoulder workout.
